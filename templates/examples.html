There are some tags special for sites so you can save time, but they are a little limited:
[ol]
[*]Wikipedia article about heat (only english wikipedia): [wiki]heat[/wiki]
[*]A google search for hear: [google]heat[/google]
[*]A word in the dictionary at dictionary.reference.com: [dict]heat[/dict]
[*]Note that the wikipedia and dictionary tags will only work correctly if the websites have a page on the word. This for example, will not work: [wiki]blabla5h7[/wiki]
[/ol]

There are some tags special for sites so you can save time, but they are a little limited:
  1. Wikipedia article about heat (only english wikipedia): heat
  2. A google search for hear: heat
  3. A word in the dictionary at dictionary.reference.com: heat
  4. Note that the wikipedia and dictionary tags will only work correctly if the websites have a page on the word. This for example, will not work: blabla5h7
